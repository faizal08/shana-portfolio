Makbool Residence

Residential | Malappuram | 2300 SQFT

About the Project

A home that celebrates its natural surroundings and creates a connection with the outdoors. Clean lines, minimalist furniture, and a neutral colour palette provide a sophisticated modern feel. Natural materials like wood and stone plastering finishes are applied throughout the exterior to ensure a cohesive, grounded look.

The design strikes a balance between warmth and comfort with sleekness and functionality. Large windows are strategically placed to blur the lines between indoors and outdoors, bringing in abundant natural light and fostering a constant, peaceful connection with nature.
