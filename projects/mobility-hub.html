About the Project

Calicut Mobility Hub is a Transit-Oriented Development designed as a solution for existing transportation challenges. Through the redevelopment and reimagining of transit infrastructure, the project aims to create a transport-friendly and convenient hub for the public.

The design creates a comfortable traveling experience by seamlessly linking different modes of transport—including monorail, buses, auto-taxis, and bicycles. This integration enables safe, secure movement and improves overall accessibility throughout the city.
