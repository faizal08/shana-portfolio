Ayurveda Medical College

Architectural Thesis Project | Calicut | 19 Acres

About the Project

Ayurveda is a system of medicine evolved in India around 600 BC. It eliminates toxic imbalances from the body and helps build resistance and maintain good health. It is a holistic approach where the mind and the body are viewed as a single entity. Kerala remains the primary center for Ayurveda and has gained significant international attention for medical tourism.

Proposed in Pantheerankavu, Calicut, with a site area of 19 acres, the goal is to design an ayurvedic medical college which will boost factors like the local economy and tourism. The design provides improved and natural health care facilities while delivering highly skilled practitioners to society.
