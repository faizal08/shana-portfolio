Sameer Residence

Residential | Vettathur | 2254 SQFT

About the Project

A home that celebrates its natural surroundings and creates a connection with the outdoors. Clean lines, minimalist furniture, and a neutral colour palette define the modern feel of this space. Natural materials like wood and stone plastering finishes are used throughout the exterior to achieve a cohesive, integrated look.

The design maintains a perfect balance between warmth and comfort with sleekness and functionality. Large windows are utilized to blur the lines between indoors and outdoors, bringing in abundant natural light and fostering a deep, continuous connection with nature.
